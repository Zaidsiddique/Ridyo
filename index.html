Welcome to BikyoApp — your go-to bike ride-sharing solution built for speed, savings, and simplicity. 0% Commission for Riders

We believe that every rupee you earn should be yours. That’s why BikyoApp charges absolutely no commission from riders. Enjoy full earnings with transparent transactions and zero hidden fees – because your hard work deserves 100% reward.

Instant Matching with Nearby Riders

No more waiting or endless searching. Our intelligent system instantly connects you with riders or drivers closest to your location. Whether you're heading to work, college, or out for a casual ride – BikyoApp makes it fast and simple.

Real-Time GPS Tracking for Every Ride

Safety and transparency are our priorities. Track your journey live, share your location with friends or family, and always stay aware. Our integrated GPS ensures smooth navigation and gives both riders and passengers peace of mind.

Cheapest Rides Compared to Other Apps

Why pay more for the same ride? BikyoApp offers the most affordable fares in the market, without compromising on comfort or safety. Our platform is designed to reduce extra charges, minimize commissions, and prioritize savings for both riders and passengers. Whether it's a short daily commute or a longer journey, BikyoApp ensures you reach your destination at the lowest possible cost – better than Ola, Uber, or any other ride-sharing app out there.
